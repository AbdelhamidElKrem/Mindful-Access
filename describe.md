### 1. **Intelligent & Intentional Blocking**
We don't just block; we facilitate **intention**.
- **The "Pause" Moment**: When you visit a distracting site (e.g., YouTube, Facebook), you aren't met with a harsh error screen. Instead, a beautiful, calming "Intention Screen" appears.

    - **Yes**: If the visit is intentional, you can choose a specific duration (15, 30, 45, or 60 mins).
    - **No**: If it was a habit, you can close the tab and return to work.
